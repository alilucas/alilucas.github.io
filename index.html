Patriots defeat Miami Dolphins on Sunday to become AFC East Champions

December 15, 2014

On Sunday, the New England Patriots defeated the Miami Dolphins 41-13 to become the AFC East Champions. With this win, the Patriots clinch their 6th AFC East concecutive title and the first NFL team to win 11 division titles in 12 years. Although having a slow first quarter, Tom Brady led his team to a victory over the Dolphins and the team could not be happier with the quick turn-around in the second half, coach Bill Belicheck commented, "We just took some not very good football and made it worse and then were able to do things a lot better in the second half." The New England Patriots move on to next Sunday, when they will face rival The New York Jets.
